My second point has already been mentioned, it concerns the minimum standards. In principle, I believe that in many cases where transport is concerned we should be working towards increased flexibility and country specific rules. However, when it comes to safety, I am rather sceptical because safety in Sweden, for example, is in principle no different from safety in Germany, Italy or Austria. I can live with these minimum standards, but I would ask the Commission to monitor the situation very carefully.
